# Manager Manual - A to Z

**Who this is for:** the station **Manager**. This guide walks you through your
whole day in order - from logging in, through reviewing the attendants' shifts
and closing off the day, to managing stock, reading reports, and administration.
Each step says **what to click** and **what to expect**.

**Your menu (top bar):** four groups - **Day**, **Stock & Sales**, **Reports**,
**Admin** - plus the **NextStop logo** (your home), a **bell** (alerts), a
**light/dark** toggle, and **Logout**.

> Money is shown in ZMW (K). Tip: the **date** you pick on your home screen is
> remembered as you move between pages, so you don't re-pick it each time.

---

## 1. Logging in

1. Open the station web address.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign in**.

**What to expect:** you land on your **home dashboard**, which includes the
**Today's Flow** card - your daily checklist and starting point.

---

## 2. Your home: "Today's Flow"

This card is the hub of your day. It shows the day's chain as four steps, each
with a status and a button:

- **Shift set up** - is there an active shift with attendants assigned.
- **Tank dips recorded** - opening/closing dips entered.
- **Handovers reviewed** - how many attendant handovers are still pending vs approved.
- **Day closed** - whether the day has been closed off.

**What each part does:**
- A coloured dot shows each step's state (done / needs attention / not done yet).
- The **button on each row** takes you straight to the page for that step
  (for example "Review" opens Handover Review; "Close-off" opens Daily Close-Off).
- The **date picker** sets the day you're working on; it carries to the other pages.

**What to expect:** at a glance you know what's done and what's next. Your normal
day is: review handovers, then close off the day. Start there.

> To return here anytime, click the **NextStop logo** (top-left).

---

## 3. Step 1 of your day - Review handovers (Day > Handover Review)

**Purpose:** check and approve each attendant's end-of-shift handover. The day
**cannot be closed** until all handovers are approved.

1. Click **Day > Handover Review** (or the **Review** button on Today's Flow).

**What you see:**
- **Summary cards** at the top: Awaiting Closing, Pending Review, Flagged,
  Approved Today.
- A **filter bar**: date, shift, and status tabs (All / Awaiting Closing /
  Pending / Flagged / Approved).
- A **table** of handovers - each row shows attendant, shift, **Expected Cash**,
  **Actual Cash**, **Difference**, any **Flags**, and the **Review Status**.

**Reviewing a handover:**
2. **Click a row** to expand it. **What to expect:** full detail opens - nozzle
   readings (electronic/mechanical, deviation), fuel/LPG/lubricant/accessory
   sales, credit sales, and the attendant's safe deposits.
3. **Approve a clean handover:** click **Approve**. **What to expect:** it is
   approved immediately and the stock is applied; the row moves to "Approved".
4. **Approve a flagged handover** (cash short or meter deviation): click
   **Approve**. **What to expect:** a box opens that **requires a written
   justification**. Use a **quick-reason chip** to fill it (for example "Counted
   twice - confirmed") or type your own, then click **Confirm Approve**. The note
   is saved to the audit trail.
5. **Return a handover** (something is wrong): click **Return**. **What to
   expect:** a box opens **requiring a reason** (reason chips available). Click
   **Confirm Return**. The attendant is notified to correct and resubmit.
6. **Approve several at once:** tick the checkboxes on clean handovers, then click
   **Approve Selected (N)**.

**Shifts still being closed:** the **Awaiting Closing** tab lists shifts whose
readings are verified but not yet closed. Use **Complete closing** to finish one.

**When everything is reviewed:** a green banner "All handovers reviewed" appears
with a **Next: Daily Close-Off** button - click it to move to Step 2.

---

## 4. Step 2 of your day - Close off the day (Day > Daily Close-Off)

**Purpose:** reconcile the day's takings against the bank deposit and **lock** the
day. This is final.

1. Click **Day > Daily Close-Off** (or the **Next: Daily Close-Off** button).

**What you see:**
- The **date** and a **status badge** (Open / Pending Reviews / Closed).
- **Approved Shifts** - a table of the day's approved handovers.
- **Daily P&L Snapshot** - fuel/LPG/lubricant/accessory revenue, expected vs
  actual cash, net variance.

2. **If any handover is still unapproved:** you will see a warning with a link
   back to **Handover Review**. **What to expect:** the close button stays
   disabled until everything is approved.
3. **Enter the bank deposit:**
   - **Deposit amount (K)** - required.
   - **Deposit reference** - optional (for example the bank slip number).
   - **Owner notes** - optional.
   **What to expect:** as you type the amount, a live "Deposit vs Actual Cash"
   line shows whether you are over, short, or exact.
4. Click **Close Day & Lock Handovers**. **What to expect:** a confirmation box
   appears stating this **locks the day and cannot be undone**. Confirm to close.

**After closing:** the day shows **Closed** with who closed it and the deposit
variance. **Close-Off History** (expandable at the bottom) lets you view or jump
to past days.

## 5. Managing stock (Stock & Sales)

Open the **Stock & Sales** menu. It contains:

### Stores / Stock
1. Click **Stock & Sales > Stores / Stock**.
**What you see:** summary cards (item count, items needing re-order, stores and
forecourt units), a re-order alert if any item is low, and a table of items with
their **Stores** and **Forecourt** quantities and re-order level.
2. To move stock, click **Manage stock** on an item. **What to expect:** one
   window opens with an **action selector** at the top - **Receive**, **Issue**,
   **Damage**, **Adjust**:
   - **Receive** - add stock into Stores (enter quantity).
   - **Issue** - move stock to the Forecourt (enter quantity).
   - **Damage** - write off damaged stock (pick the bin, quantity, and a
     **required reason** - reason chips available).
   - **Adjust** - set the counted quantity after a physical count (pick bin, enter
     the new quantity, and a **required reason**).
   Switching the action resets the quantity/reason. Click **Confirm** to save.
3. **Add or edit an item:** click **+ Add / Edit Item** (top right).
4. **See history:** click **Show recent movements** for the stock ledger.

### Stock Takes
1. Click **Stock & Sales > Stock Takes**.
**What to expect:** start a count, enter the counted quantities for each item, and
**Submit**. Submitting applies the count; the **owner approves** it.

### Tank Levels / Sales / Credit Accounts
- **Tank Levels** - current fuel volumes per tank (view).
- **Sales** - sales figures and trends (view).
- **Credit Accounts** - manage credit customers and their limits.

---

## 6. Reviewing reports (Reports)

Open the **Reports** menu. These are read-only views (most can be exported):
- **Three-Way Reconciliation** - physical vs meter vs accounting comparison.
- **Tank Analysis** - tank movement and possible loss/variance.
- **Shift Reconciliation** - per-shift reconciliation detail.
- **Sales Reports** - sales summaries and trends.
- **Tank Readings & Monitor** - historical tank readings and validation.
- **Advanced Reports** - staff/nozzle/island/product and custom reports.
- **Anomaly Alerts** - flagged discrepancies needing attention.
- **Notifications** - system notifications (low stock, overdue deposits, etc.).

**What to expect:** pick a date range or filter where offered, then view or
export. None of these change data.

---

## 7. Administration (Admin)

Open the **Admin** menu.

### Settings
1. Click **Admin > Settings**.
**What you can do:**
- **Fuel prices** - change a price (takes effect immediately) or **schedule** a
  change for a future date/time.
- **Tax / levy rates**.
- **Validation thresholds** - for example cash-shortage tolerance, meter-deviation
  percentage.
- **Reconciliation tolerances**.
**What to expect:** changes are saved and recorded in the Audit Log; price changes
apply to new sales from their effective time.

### Users
1. Click **Admin > Users**.
**What to expect:** create, edit, or deactivate user accounts and set their role
(attendant, supervisor, manager).

### Audit Log
1. Click **Admin > Audit Log**.
**What to expect:** a searchable record of every change - price updates, approvals,
returns, settings, user changes - filterable by action, type, user, and date.

---

## 8. Logging out

Click **Logout** (top-right). You return to the login screen.

---

## Quick reference - the manager's day

1. **Log in** and land on **Today's Flow**.
2. **Day > Handover Review** - approve the clean ones, justify or return the rest,
   then click **Next: Daily Close-Off**.
3. **Day > Daily Close-Off** - enter the **bank deposit**, then **Close Day & Lock**.
4. As needed: **Stock & Sales** (receive/issue/damage/adjust, stock takes),
   **Reports** (reconciliation, sales, anomalies), **Admin** (settings, users,
   audit).

**Rules to remember**
- You **cannot close the day** until **all handovers are approved**.
- **Flagged approvals** and **returns** require a **written note** (kept in the
  audit trail).
- **Closing the day is final** - it locks all handovers for that date.
- A **stock take** is submitted by you and **approved by the owner**.
